Welcome to Italy!

Over this past summer, I studied abroad in France, but over this 3 month period, I took 3 trips to Italy in many different cities. See all my top recommendations below!

Rome

Explore the ancient ruins of Rome, including the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Pantheon.

Venice

Enjoy the romantic canals of Venice, and don't miss a gondola ride through the winding waterways.

Florence

Visit Florence to see stunning Renaissance art and architecture, including the famous Florence Cathedral.

Amalfi Coast

Relax on the beautiful beaches of the Amalfi Coast and explore charming coastal villages.
